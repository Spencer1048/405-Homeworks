The Lovers

A union of harmony, full of trust, confidence and strength. This relationship is one that represents both a physical attraction and a deep emotional bond between them. It can also represent an inner harmony between two aspects of one's personality.

The Tower

Inevitable destruction and the breakdown of something in our lives that we consider foundational. But there is always an upside - when the tower levels, not only do we uncover out own hidden resillience, but we can also rebuild anew, with greater inner insight.

Death

A cycle comes to an end, a radical transformation that requires a part of oneself to be sacrificed to be able to continue to the next plan. With loss, something new can begin. Death carries a scyth not just to destroy but also to sow the new harvest.
